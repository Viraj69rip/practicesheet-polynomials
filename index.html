Reason – if p(x) doesn't intersect x-axis, it has no zeroes.

Q. 1. Find zeroes of p(t) = 20t – 16t²
p(t) = 4t(5 – 4t) = 0 t = 0 or 5 – 4t = 0 → t = 5/4 Zeroes: 0 and 5/4
Q. 2. Which graph represents p(t)? Justify.
p(t) = –16t² + 20t. Coefficient of t² is negative. So parabola opens downward (∩ shape). At t=0, h=0 (starts at water). At t=5/4, h=0 (returns). Graph (b) is correct.
Q. 3. Value of h at t = 3/2 sec? Interpret.
OR: Distance covered before hitting water again?
h = 20(3/2) – 16(3/2)² = 30 – 36 = –6 h = –6 cm (negative) → dolphin is below water level. It had re-entered water before t = 3/2 sec. h = –6 cm (below water level) OR: Dolphin hits water at t = 5/4. Distance = 20 × 5/4 = 25 cm
